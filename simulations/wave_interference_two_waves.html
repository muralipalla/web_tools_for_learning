What to observe

Wave 1: y₁
Wave 2: y₂
Resultant: y₁ + y₂
y₁ = A₁ sin(2πx/λ₁ − 2πf₁t + φ₁)
y₂ = A₂ sin(2πx/λ₂ − 2πf₂t + φ₂)
y = y₁ + y₂

When the two waves have the same wavelength and frequency, phase controls whether they reinforce or cancel. With different frequencies, you will see a changing interference pattern.

  • Same phase → constructive interference.
  • Opposite phase → destructive interference.
  • Different frequencies → time-dependent beating-like patterns.
