Hydrogen Orbital Probability Visualizer

Interactive visualization of \(|\psi_{n,l,m}(r,\theta,\phi)|^2\) using Three.js.

HTML + Three.js

Quantum numbers

Allowed values: \(n = 1,2,3,\ldots\), \(l=0\ldots n-1\), \(m=-l\ldots +l\).

Visualization

Orientation

3p, m = 0 Preparing orbital…
positive phase negative phase probability density

Formula used

\[\psi_{n,l,m}(r,\theta,\phi)=R_{n,l}(r)Y_l^m(\theta,\phi)\]

\[\rho = \frac{2r}{n a_0},\quad a_0=1\text{ in this demo}\]

Cloud points are sampled from \(|\psi|^2\) in 3D volume.

The surface is a teaching-friendly angular envelope. The cloud is the better representation of full probability density and radial nodes.

Radial distribution

Plot of \(r^2 |R_{n,l}(r)|^2\), normalized to its own maximum.

Angular distribution

Heatmap of \(|Y_l^m(\theta,\phi)|^2\). Horizontal: \(\phi\), vertical: \(\theta\).

Use mouse drag to rotate camera, scroll to zoom, right-drag to pan. Use orientation sliders to rotate the orbital itself.
