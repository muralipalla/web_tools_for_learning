Black Body Radiation and Radiative Balance

A pedagogical animation: atoms vibrate thermally, energetic atoms emit expanding wavefronts, emission cools them, and incoming solar plane waves supply energy. The aim is to visualize radiative balance, not to calculate an exact Planck spectrum.

What this demonstrates

A warm body does not store energy indefinitely. As atoms vibrate more strongly, the body emits radiation. Emission removes energy. If sunlight supplies energy at about the same rate as radiation removes it, the average temperature remains approximately steady.
