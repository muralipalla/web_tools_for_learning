Before you begin

Use the usual oxidation-number rules. For some compounds the question asks for the average oxidation number.

Free element:
oxidation number = 0
Group 1 metals:
usually +1
Oxygen:
usually −2; peroxide −1; superoxide −1/2
Fluorine:
always −1 in compounds
